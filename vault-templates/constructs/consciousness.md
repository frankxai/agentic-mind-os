# Consciousness

> The quality of awareness and present-moment experience — what it's like to be you, right now, attending to this.
> This note is a lens, not a test — a place to file what you notice, and the frame agents
> use to read your daily notes.

## What to notice (for your notes)
- Moments of full presence vs. running on autopilot.
- Awareness of awareness — when you caught yourself in the act.
- States: focused, foggy, flow, scattered, still.

## Related constructs
- `attention` — awareness and attention are tightly coupled.
- `metacognition` — meta-awareness is consciousness turned on itself.
- `emotion` — affective tone colors the felt quality of experience.
- `identity` — the continuous self is felt, not just remembered.
