# Social Cognition

> How you perceive, interpret, and reason about other people and your relationships with them.
> This note is a lens, not a test — a place to file what you notice, and the frame agents
> use to read your daily notes.

## What to notice (for your notes)
- Interactions that stuck, and what you read into them.
- Where you assumed another's intent (and whether you checked it).
- Relationships that gave energy vs. drained it this week.

## Related constructs
- `identity` — self is partly built in relation to others.
- `emotion` — much affect is social in origin.
- `belief` — beliefs about others drive how you treat them.
- `behavior` — social context shapes what you do.
