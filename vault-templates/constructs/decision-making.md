# Decision-Making

> How you weigh options, commit, and act under uncertainty — and how you judge the choice afterward.
> This note is a lens, not a test — a place to file what you notice, and the frame agents
> use to read your daily notes.

## What to notice (for your notes)
- Choices made this week, and what you weighed (or didn't).
- Decisions avoided or deferred — and what that cost.
- Process vs. outcome: a good process can have a bad outcome and still be good.

## Related constructs
- `belief` — beliefs set the priors a decision runs on.
- `emotion` — affect quietly weights options.
- `behavior` — the decision is executed (or contradicted) by what you do.
- `metacognition` — review the process, not only the result.
