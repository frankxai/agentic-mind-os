# Behavior

> What you actually do — the observable actions, habits, and routines that carry intention into the world.
> This note is a lens, not a test — a place to file what you notice, and the frame agents
> use to read your daily notes.

## What to notice (for your notes)
- What you did vs. what you intended (the gap is data).
- Habits and routines, including the ones running on autopilot.
- Triggers and contexts that reliably produce an action.

## Related constructs
- `motivation` — the why behind the what.
- `decision-making` — behavior executes (or contradicts) the choice.
- `attention` — environment design shapes behavior via attention.
- `identity` — repeated behavior becomes self-concept.
