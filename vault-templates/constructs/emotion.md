# Emotion

> Affective states that color experience, signal what matters, and bias attention, memory, and decision-making.
> This note is a lens, not a test — a place to file what you notice, and the frame agents
> use to read your daily notes.

## What to notice (for your notes)
- What landed this week — good, hard, surprising — and what it was about.
- Recurring affective tones across days (a low hum, a spike, a relief).
- What a feeling was *pointing at* — emotions are signal, not noise.

## Related constructs
- `attention` — mood biases what you orient toward (attentional bias).
- `memory` — strong affect tags memories (flashbulb, mood-congruent recall).
- `motivation` — feeling is upstream of wanting.
- `decision-making` — affect quietly weights options.
