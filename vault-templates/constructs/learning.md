# Learning

> The acquisition of knowledge, skills, and adaptations through experience and practice.
> This note is a lens, not a test — a place to file what you notice, and the frame agents
> use to read your daily notes.

## What to notice (for your notes)
- What you'd want to still know in a month (candidate for retention).
- Where understanding clicked vs. where it stayed fuzzy — fuzzy means re-encode.
- A skill that moved, even slightly, and what moved it.

## Related constructs
- `memory` — retrieval practice is the highest-leverage learning move.
- `metacognition` — knowing how you learn improves what you learn.
- `motivation` — you learn what you have a reason to.
- `attention` — no encoding without attention first.
