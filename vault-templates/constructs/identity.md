# Identity

> The sense of self over time — values, roles, narrative, and the story you tell about who you are.
> This note is a lens, not a test — a place to file what you notice, and the frame agents
> use to read your daily notes.

## What to notice (for your notes)
- Beliefs about yourself that showed up in the notes ('I'm someone who...').
- Tension between roles, or between a value and an action.
- Shifts in the self-narrative, even small ones.

## Related constructs
- `memory` — autobiographical memory is part of self.
- `belief` — self-beliefs are a special, sticky kind of belief.
- `motivation` — motives rooted in identity hold longer.
- `social-cognition` — identity is partly built in relation to others.
