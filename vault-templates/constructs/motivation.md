# Motivation

> The drives, goals, and reasons that initiate, direct, and sustain behavior.
> This note is a lens, not a test — a place to file what you notice, and the frame agents
> use to read your daily notes.

## What to notice (for your notes)
- What you actually moved toward vs. what you said you'd move toward.
- Where energy came easily and where it stalled.
- Intrinsic pull (the thing itself) vs. extrinsic push (the consequence).

## Related constructs
- `attention` — you sustain focus on what you've got a reason for.
- `behavior` — motivation is the why behind the what.
- `identity` — motives rooted in who you are hold longer.
- `emotion` — feeling is upstream of wanting.
